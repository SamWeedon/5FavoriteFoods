My Top 5 Favorite Meals

1. Chicken Parmesan

Chicken parmesan offers a delightfully unique combination of crispiness, cheesiness, and sauciness that simply cannot be replicated.

2. Cheeseburger

Especially when paired with fries or potato wedges, nothing is more comforting than biting into a juicy homemade cheeseburger with all of the fixings.

3. Turkey Club

A nice cold, and somehow refreshing turkey club is exactly what is needed after the first half of the day is up and the lunch bell has rung.
Pair it with some salty chips and a lemonade, please!

4. Medium-Rare Steak

Nothing in food is more primal than my love for bloody steaks, which satisfy an urge that cannot be explained. A good sear is key.

5. Tuna Steak

While fish usually is not my favorite meal, tuna steak adds a certain level of density and meatiness that typical fish is not able to replicate.
Marinated in soy sauce with a good sear and rice on the side is how I like it.
